My Work Rolls

My Waiter Roles

  • Customer Service:

    Waiters are primarily responsible for providing excellent customer service. This includes greeting customers, taking orders, answering questions about the menu, and ensuring that customers have a pleasant dining experience.

  • Order Management:

    Waiters manage orders by communicating them to the kitchen staff, ensuring that the orders are prepared correctly, and serving them to the customers in a timely manner.

  • Table Maintenance:

    They are also responsible for setting up tables, refilling water glasses, clearing dishes, and ensuring that the dining area is clean and presentable.

  • Upselling:

    Waiters often engage in upselling by suggesting additional menu items, desserts, or drinks to enhance the customer's experience and increase sales.

My waiter roles require excellent communication skills, attention to detail, the ability to work in a fast-paced environment, and a focus on providing exceptional service to customers.


My Bartender Roles

  • Mixing Drinks:

    Bartenders specialize in preparing and serving alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. They must have knowledge of various cocktails, spirits, and drink recipes to fulfill customer orders.

  • Inventory Management:

    Bartenders are responsible for managing the bar's inventory, including ordering supplies, maintaining stock levels, and monitoring product quality.

  • Customer Interaction:

    They interact with customers at the bar, taking drink orders, providing recommendations, and engaging in conversation to create a friendly and welcoming atmosphere.

  • Cash Handling:

    Bartenders handle cash and credit card transactions, process payments accurately, and maintain a secure cash register.
